Frequently asked questions

How fast can you turn around a project?
Simple projects complete in 1–2 weeks. Complex, multi‑market work varies. We’ll share a timeline in your quote.
Can you target niche B2B audiences?
Yes. We source via vetted expert networks and panels. Feasibility and pricing depend on role seniority and geography.
What tools do you use?
We’re platform‑agnostic and can use your preferred stack. We commonly work with Qualtrics, Typeform, SurveyMonkey, and custom dashboards.
Do you provide raw data?
Absolutely. We deliver raw CSV/XLSX, codebook, and summary decks. Enterprise can add dashboards and APIs.
How do you handle privacy?
We follow a GDPR‑first approach: data minimization, DPAs, optional EU hosting, and role‑based access. We avoid collecting unnecessary PII.
